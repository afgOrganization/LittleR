Upload Your Receipt


When you make a purchase of a participating product, take a picture of your receipt on your mobile device and submit it by clicking the "UPLOAD" button. The receipt image is automatically uploaded and upon verification, points are awarded Alternatively, you can upload your receipt image to your computer and submit it online through you desktop browser.


Supported retailers include: CVS, Dollar General, Kroger, Walgreens, and Walmart


You can earn points for these various products:

  • • 500 Points: Nasal and saline products
  • • 1000 Points: Fever products
  • • 1500 Points: Cough and cold products
  • • 2000 Points: Gas and griipe water products

To ensure that you're rewarded for your purchase, follow these steps when taking an image of your receipt. We need to be able to cearly read the retailer, the item(s) on the receipt, and the receipt number.


  • • Place the receipt on a flat surface witha solid background, and smooth it out so that it lays flat.
  • • Capture all of the transactional data in your image. You can omit any surveys or special offers at the top or bottom of the receipt.
  • • Make sure the image is centered and straight.
  • • The image should be crisp, not blurry.
  • • Take the image as close as possible.
  • • Take and upload the highest resolution image possible. On an iPhone, us the "actual" image.

If your receipt is badly printed (light ink, marks obscuring text, etc.), please contact customer service at [email] for assistance with your receipt.
